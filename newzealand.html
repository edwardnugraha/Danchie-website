Design of an exhibition space for New Zealand Central in Shanghai. The project involved creating a versatile area to display various New Zealand products, following the client's brand guidelines. The design featured themes of black and wood, using natural tones and textures, and prioritized light and space. It included focal points for honey and natural products, and used simple graphics and geometrical shapes inspired by New Zealand. The exhibition was well-received and considered a success.
