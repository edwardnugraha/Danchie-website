Danchie was commissioned by Anta to design a gamified pop-up store with easy setup and reusability, centered around Anta's Flashfoam technology for cushioned running shoes. The concept seamlessly integrated the innovative footwear technology into interactive game experiences, ensuring brand immersion. Danchie's design emphasized modular structures and lightweight materials for effortless assembly and disassembly, facilitating future reuse across various locations.

In tackling the constraints of limited space for the pop-up store, our primary mission was to craft innovative and engaging game concepts capable of not only capturing attention but also fostering brand recognition and customer interaction. This challenge necessitated a creative approach, where we delved deep into the essence of the brand, understanding its values, target audience, and desired impact. Through meticulous brainstorming and strategic planning, we sought to develop games that would not only entertain but also leave a lasting impression on visitors, ensuring they not only remembered the experience but also associated it directly with the brand.
